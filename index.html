A New Career Awaits!

My career experiences and skills have grown from my childhood hobbies - I was an avid reader, a writer, an illustrator, thinker, and a tinkerer. From a young age, I always tried to discover how things worked by taking them apart and assembling back together again, often to the chagrin of my elders. But such curiosity served me well; I turned it toward academic pursuits where I earned a dual major in Philosophy and English.

I started out as a teacher and later moved into fundraising, where I worked for over a decade raising money for scholarships and bringing together students and alumni.
